About us

WHY VBR Engineering Industries

VBR Engg is a one of the Fastest Growing and leading Steel Structural Products manufacturing & Maintenance company in India.

VBR Engg is a Specialized in Design, Engineering, Construction, Fabrication, Grit Blasting, Painting and Erection for the Residential & Non-Residential Construction, Defense, Oil & Gas, Refineries, Petrochemicals, Power, Water treatment and various other industrial sectors.

VBR Engg has a full-fledged facility of 2000m2 and located in Mobhela Industrial Estate for Steel Structural Products manufacturing, Remanufacturing, repair & maintenance services.

The company is deeply rooted in India and is committed towards developing the local industry and continually improving the quality and technology of its businesses.

VBR Engg has grown from strength, diversifying its business between the specialized maintenance process equipment and executing service contracts and projects.

Value Engineering

Taking advantage of the collective experience of our team members, VBR Engg can provide Value Engineering to its client, to reduce expenditures and determine reliable options that will improve the project's cost-effectiveness. A specialized & experienced technical team in the company gets involved early on with the consultants & client engineer's, to work together for recommending alternative materials and construction methods that will keep the project on budget, while ensuring the aesthetic and functional intentions of the design specifications.

Adept Maintenance

The company has built-up a strong workforce specializing in post-project services, because we strongly believe that when the construction scope is completed, the work done is only an implementation of Architect's vision. It takes an engineering expertise and devotion to do an adequate maintenance and make sure the system operates at its peak for a long time

Advance Infrastructure

The major assets of the company consist state-of- the-art construction equipment’s & machineries, along with the wide-ranging shuttering systems. All the Plant & Machinery is maintained by a dedicated team with extreme care, ensuring safety, dependability and performance thereof. The company has implemented ERP system for budgeting, Finance, Accounting, procurement, asset management, Human resource & project management. It enables high accuracy of data and timely availability of key inputs for decision making, sites are inter-connected with ERP software for managing all operational aspects.
